About me

Machine Learning researcher in London with a large interest in startups. Previously, part of Entrepreneur First and co-founder of ML startup Millennials.ai in Amsterdam. Spent time at Onfido working on out-of-distribution detection and deep generative models at the University of Amsterdam prior to that. Research interests include Generative Modelling, Bayesian Inference, OOD detection and a keen interest in most of the practical components of deploying models in production.
